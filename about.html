Our Vision

To create a world where every child with special needs, regardless of socioeconomic background, has the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential within an inclusive educational system.

Our Mission

  • Empower Children: Provide children with special needs in low-income communities with the resources and support they need to succeed academically, socially, and emotionally.
  • Train Teachers: Equip educators with the knowledge, skills, and strategies to effectively teach and support children with diverse learning needs.
  • Advocate for Inclusion: Promote policies and practices that ensure inclusivity and accessibility within educational systems.

Project Goals

  • Increase enrollment and attendance of children with special needs in low-income schools.
  • Improve academic performance and social-emotional well-being of children with special needs.
  • Increase the capacity of teachers to effectively teach and support children with special needs.
  • Raise awareness about the needs and rights of children with special needs within communities.
  • Create a sustainable model for inclusive education that can be replicated in other low-income communities.

Target Audience

  • Children with Special Needs: Including children with disabilities, learning differences, and developmental delays.
  • Teachers: Especially educators in low-income schools working with diverse learning needs.
  • Parents and Families: Engaged through support, guidance, and resources.

Core Values

  • Authenticity
  • Understanding
  • Resilience
  • Opportunity
  • Responsibility
  • Aspiration
